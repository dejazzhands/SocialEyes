Dining Out

Definition

"to dine away from home, esp in a restaurant. (foll by on) to have dinner at the expense of someone else mainly for the sake of one's knowledge or conversation about (a subject or story)"

Start the steps diagram

Start
Decide on a place to eat. Enter the building: leave the door open if other people walk in with you
Ask for a seat, or sit anywhere you like if it's free seating.
Order directly at the register, saying the order name and number on the menu. A cashier will take your order and process your payment before food is served.
Wait for the food to be ready. It is usually placed in a pickup area close to the kitchen, and someone would call out your order.
Eat your food once you bring it to a table.
Once you are finished, make sure to clean up after yourself, putting plates in the used tray and sorting out waste in the trash bin.
Leave the diner once you are ready.
