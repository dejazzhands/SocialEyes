Dining Out

Definition

"to dine away from home, esp in a restaurant. (foll by on) to have dinner at the expense of someone else mainly for the sake of one's knowledge or conversation about (a subject or story)"

Start the steps diagram

Start
Enter the building: leave the door open if other people walk in with you
Approach a staff member (waiter or waitress) and ask for a seat
When the staff member gives you the menu, look for a food you want to eat and a drink to go with it
Raise your hand to get a staff member's attention. Tell them what food and drink you want
Eat your food once the staff bring it to your table
Raise your hand to ask for the bill
Give your CREDIT or DEBIT card if you want to pay with a card. Alternatively, give the staff member the amount of money they asked for. Remember to say THANK YOU
Walk towards the door after you paid. Don't forget to open the door for other people to be polite
